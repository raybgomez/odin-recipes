Puerto Rican Mofongo

Let's Make a Delicious Mofongo!

A traditional Puerto Rican dish I've eaten since childhood. My mother would cook this for us on special occasion. You'll come to love this dish with its garlicy taste and crunchy texture.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
  • 4 green plantains
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 pound pork crackling (fried pork skin)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 stick of butter

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a small pot or saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Cut plantains into about 2cm slices and fry the slices till golden brown all over (3-5 mins). Remove from the oil and place on a paper towel.
  3. Using a large mortar and pestle, crush the garlic and salt together, add in plantains - mash, then add pork cracklings - mash together with the plantains
  4. Shape the mixture into a large ball and place each in a bowl.
  5. Add a side of melted butter mixed with garlic to use as a sauce to eat with your mofongo.
